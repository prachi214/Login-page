Gulab Jamun

In gredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 tsp milk
  • 1 1/2 cups khoya, grated
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup maida

Recipe

  1. Take required quantity of sugar and water in a deep pan.
  2. Now add milk and cardamom seeds to the sugar water.
  3. Then add saffron and cardamom powder.
  4. Take the grated Khoya in a medium size deep bowl.
  5. Now add milk and cardamom seeds to the sugar water.
  6. Divide the dough into equal parts.
